Meet Shaldrine Oside

Shaldrine Oside is a talented crochet designer from Kakamega, Kenya, who has been crafting beautiful handmade pieces for over 10 years. Her passion for crocheting has always been self driven and motivated , and she has since developed her own unique style that combines traditional techniques with modern designs.

Each piece is carefully crafted with attention to detail and quality, ensuring that every customer receives a unique and beautiful item that will last for years to come. Shaldrine's work has been featured in local fashion shows and has gained recognition for its exceptional craftsmanship.
